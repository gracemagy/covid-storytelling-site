Circuit breaker, heartbreaker?

Stripping residents of their personal freedoms had resulted in increased mental stress in homes.

The 'Circuit Breaker' (CB) measures had been announced by the Singapore government on April 3 2020 in response to the growing number of new cases. The first phase required residents to stay at home to curb the pandemic.
